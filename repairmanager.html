Managing Repair Services During a Claim
Problem Statement

As part of the transition to create a fully digital claims experience, the customer needed a solution to indicate a need for water clean up services and later how they prefer to get an inspection of their home. Since there was no prior digital solution, I was able to create the experience with few constraints beyond our legal and brand requirements.

Ideation and Design

What started as a simple form design soon morphed as business requirements shifted and different aspects were re-prioritized. One of the larger hurdles was ensuring that insurance jargon was reduced to a minimum to reduce the customer's cognitive burden.

Results

After many rounds of critique and design, business injects, and development constraints, I was able to create a simple form flow that guides the customer through the initial phase of the claim process and puts them on track to getting back to normal.
