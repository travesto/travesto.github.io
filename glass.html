Auto Glass Claims Vision Work
Problem Statement

While most customers have a postive experience with the Glass claim experience, there was room for improvement. At the time, the vast majority of these claims were being filed via phone. My business partners tasked us with finding ways our 3rd party vendor could improve and looking at what a potential in-house solution could look like.

Early Findings

Since this product is hosted by a 3rd party, we had to be creative in our research. We partnered with our SEO and Data Analytics team to gleam any relevant data beyond what the 3rd party was willing to share. In addition with stakeholder interviews, on-site interviews at the vendor's shop, we were able to put together mental models and an experience map to represent our customers' experience when filing glass claims.

The intial research indicated most customers were apprehensive about filing due to unknown costs, unaswered questions, and scheduling problems.

Ideation and Design

Based on the research we conducted, we began looking at potential near and far term solutions. We created a bevy of concepts with accompaning illustrations to help our business partners envision a better glass claim experience.

Results

Our business parters agreeed that despite the difficulty of covnincing the vendor to implement changes, there were many beneficial changes that could be made. Currently our business partners are waiting for development resources before moving forward with some of our proposed concepts.
